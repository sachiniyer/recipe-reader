# Chicken Curry

## Ingredients
- 2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into 1-inch pieces
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 1 large onion, finely chopped
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tablespoons ginger, freshly grated
- 2 tablespoons curry powder
- 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1 teaspoon ground coriander
- 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
- 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)
- 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
- 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
- 1 cup chicken broth
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 2 tablespoons fresh cilantro, chopped
- Cooked rice for serving

## Instructions
1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium-high heat.
2. Season chicken with salt and pepper, then brown in batches, about 3-4 minutes per batch. Remove and set aside.
3. In the same pot, add onion and sauté until soft, about 5 minutes.
4. Add garlic and ginger, cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
5. Stir in curry powder, cumin, coriander, turmeric, and cayenne. Toast spices for 30 seconds.
6. Add coconut milk, diced tomatoes, and chicken broth, stirring to combine.
7. Return chicken to the pot and bring to a simmer.
8. Reduce heat to low, cover, and cook for 30 minutes until chicken is tender.
9. Uncover and simmer for another 10-15 minutes until sauce thickens slightly.
10. Adjust seasoning with salt and pepper.
11. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve over rice.
