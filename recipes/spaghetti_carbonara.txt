# Spaghetti Carbonara

## Ingredients
- 1 pound spaghetti
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 8 oz pancetta or guanciale, diced
- 4 large eggs
- 1 cup freshly grated Pecorino Romano cheese
- 1/2 cup freshly grated Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese
- Freshly ground black pepper
- Salt to taste
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

## Instructions
1. Bring a large pot of salted water to boil and cook spaghetti until al dente.
2. While pasta is cooking, heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
3. Add pancetta and sauté until crispy, about 3-5 minutes.
4. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, both cheeses, and plenty of black pepper.
5. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water, then drain pasta.
6. Working quickly, add hot pasta to the skillet with pancetta, tossing to combine.
7. Remove skillet from heat and add egg mixture, tossing constantly with tongs until sauce is creamy (not scrambled).
8. Add a splash of reserved pasta water if needed to loosen the sauce.
9. Season with salt to taste and garnish with parsley.
10. Serve immediately with additional grated cheese and black pepper.
